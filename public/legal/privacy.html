Privacy Policy

Last updated: [DATE]

This Privacy Policy describes what we collect, how we use it, and what choices you have. We built M & G to be privacy-respecting: most data is either never stored or stored only as long as needed to keep the Service safe.

What we collect

  • IP address — used for matchmaking, rate-limiting, moderation, and enforcing bans. IP addresses are stored alongside reports and ban records.
  • Interest tags — the optional keywords you enter to match with similar users. These are transient and are not stored against your identity after a session ends.
  • Text messages — relayed through our servers to deliver them to the other party. Messages are not stored in a durable database under normal operation; transient logs may be kept briefly for abuse prevention.
  • Video and audio — streamed peer-to-peer via WebRTC. Video and audio streams never touch our servers and are not recorded by us.
  • Report submissions — when you report another user, we store the report content, timestamp, and the IP address of the reported user so moderators can review and act on it.
  • Basic technical logs — such as connection timestamps, user agent, and error data, used to operate and debug the Service.

Cookies and local storage

We use browser localStorage to remember that you acknowledged the age gate (randomchat_age_ok). We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking cookies.

How we use your data

  • To match you with other users.
  • To moderate the Service and enforce our Terms of Service.
  • To investigate abuse and prevent repeat offenders from returning.
  • To comply with legal obligations, respond to lawful requests, and cooperate with law enforcement.

Third parties

  • STUN/TURN servers — used to establish peer-to-peer connections. These relay connection metadata and, when direct peer connections fail, encrypted media.
  • Hosting provider — hosts our signaling server and receives the technical data necessary to operate it.
  • Analytics — if enabled, a privacy-respecting analytics tool may collect aggregate usage metrics. No personal identifiers are shared.

Data retention

  • Reports and ban records: retained for up to 1 year, or longer if required for an active law-enforcement investigation.
  • Session logs: retained for up to 30 days.
  • Transient data (interest tags, match queue state): discarded when the session ends.

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights under laws such as the GDPR (EU/UK) or CCPA/CPRA (California), including the right to access, correct, or delete personal data we hold about you, and the right to object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise these rights, email privacy@[yourdomain].com. Because we do not require accounts, you may need to provide the IP address and approximate timestamps of your sessions so we can locate relevant records.

Children

The Service is strictly for adults 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children. Any content depicting or soliciting minors is reported to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) and to law enforcement. If you believe a child has used M & G, contact us immediately.

Security

We use industry-standard safeguards to protect the data we store, but no system is perfectly secure. You use the Service at your own risk.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.

Contact
